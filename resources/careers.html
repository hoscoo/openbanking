We're doing
big things

Hoscoo is powering the shift to a cardless society. Our team of enthusiastic payments and fintech experts is growing quickly!

Join Us

Who we are

We are leaders from the top Fintech and Payments companies leading the Open Banking movement. Hoscoo is a culturally diverse team from over 50 nationalities and values its international team as an asset to developing and delivering innovative, high-quality, global products.

Owners

We act like owners. We are empowered and take accountability for driving exceptional results. We applaud excellence and speed.

Collaborators

We help and challenge one another. We are direct, respectful and caring. We embrace diversity. We act with integrity and avoid personal agendas.

Customer-Centered

We exist to serve consumers, merchants and banks. We simplify incredibly complex challenges and create innovative, trusted payment solutions.

What We Do

Our digital account-to-account platform redefines the speed, simplicity, and security of payments. Consumers pay for purchases by simply signing into their bank accounts, bypassing the card networks, and never leaving a merchant’s site or app – no credit card numbers or separate account setup necessary.

Remote First

Flexible remote option means no commute and office mates of your choosing!

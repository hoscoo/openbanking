Meet Hoscoo

At Hoscoo, we connect top brands, drawing on our experience from leading top global brands. Our team consists of experts with backgrounds at renowned brands: Huawei, Swisscom, Etisalat, Amazon, Zain, IBM, SAP, Dukascopy Bank, Pepperstone & Fusion Market.

Our Mission

Leading the Open Banking revolution

Our account-to-account platform redefines the speed, simplicity and security of payments, letting consumers pay merchants directly from their bank accounts.

By establishing direct connections with financial institutions, we are removing complexity and high-costs and adding efficiency and accessibility to the payments ecosystem. Our modern, pay-with-bank infrastructure allows Hoscoo to handle the complete payment experience, from initiation to collections.

Our platform powers guaranteed payments, instant payouts, KYC capabilities, and data to supercharge financial experiences and delight consumers.

Our history

How we got here

Rodgers John Massawe founded Hoscoo Open Banking with a clear vision: to create a payment system that was not only secure and convenient but also accessible to everyone, everywhere. The journey began with Rodgers dedicating himself to the front-end development, laying the crucial foundation for what would eventually evolve into a global solution. Understanding the importance of collaboration, he assembled a team of high-end international experts and investors. Together, they expanded the vision of Hoscoo, connecting top brands with millions of consumers worldwide, ensuring the platform would cater to a global audience and revolutionize the way people engage with banking and financial transactions.

As the Founder and CEO of Hoscoo Open Banking, Rodgers is spearheading the development of a revolutionary platform that transforms global payments by enabling secure, real-time account-to-account transactions powered by advanced AI and biometric technologies. This experience provided him with a strong foundation in large-scale software development, systems integration, and strategic project management, all of which he now leverages in his entrepreneurial ventures. With a global perspective, Rodgers founded Hoscoo to address the challenges of traditional payment systems and to create a more seamless, cost-effective solution for businesses and consumers worldwide. His deep understanding of both the technical and financial aspects of open banking has positioned Hoscoo as a leader in the fintech industry, with the potential to disrupt and redefine how transactions are conducted on a global scale. He has further honed his expertise through a professional course in AI & ML from Harvard, enabling him to integrate cutting-edge technology into innovative financial solutions.

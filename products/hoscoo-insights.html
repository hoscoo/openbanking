How it Works

Assess Financial Health in Real-Time

Hoscoo’s proprietary, API-based Open Banking platform pulls real-time transaction data from bank accounts. Our consistent and reliable data allows merchants to inspect spending hygiene, account balance history, NSF risk, and other parameters to simplify the credit application process and make better-informed credit decisions.

Benefits

Faster underwriting assessments

Utilize up-to-date transaction and account data to build a holistic view of financial health and improve decision-making.

Leverage clear and accurate bank-grade data instantly

Build confidence in credit decisions and reduce risk by leveraging reliable data in real-time.

Build a comprehensive view of consumer financial health

Compile an account summary with up to 90 days of transaction data, including average balance, NSF histories, overdraft occurrences, and more.

Data compilation and categorization for easy decision-making

Utilize machine learning rules to make sense of retrieved financial data, ensuring accurate credit decisions.

Optimize application completion rate

Direct connectivity via Open Banking eliminates the need for tedious and time-consuming document collection.

Future-proofed risk profiling

Minimize risk and subsequent cash flow issues by verifying a customer’s account balance history before approving an application.
